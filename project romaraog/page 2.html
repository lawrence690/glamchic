About Us GLAM CHIC

Kacylyn Mapalad started in 2020 with the goal of meeting all of our customers' beauty needs in one place, saving them time, and enhancing their self-esteem.

GLAM CHIC was a small business that offered lip therapy at the time, such as lip gloss with a cooling sensation when applied. The goods of this cosmetics line now include matte lip, brow pomade, and certain lip tints, a groundbreaking idea in the Glam Chic of mid-2020. Glam Chic is now a real haven for beauty. an environment that honors internal beauty. a place with skin care, makeup, and professional personnel to help you on your quest for beauty. Everyone who makes a purchase from our online store is treated like a member of our family. Every time you acquire a product from us, we want you to feel special, at home, cherished, welcomed, and comprehended..
